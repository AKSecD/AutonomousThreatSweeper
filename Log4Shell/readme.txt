Securonix has been actively monitoring and investigating the critical Log4j/Log4Shell Zero-Day Vulnerability (CVE-2021-44228) to help our teams and our customers detect, mitigate, and respond. This repository is provided to Securonix Autonomous Threat Sweep service subscribers, where new or existing indicators of compromise identified by Securonix that are related to this vulnerability and Spotter Queries are hosted on-demand for your reference, and will be updated regularly as new intelligence is identified by the team. 

The latest information Securonix has on the vulnerability, including content updates, mitigation recommendations, and additional observations, can be found on our website. https://www.securonix.com/blog/log4j-log4shell-zero-day-vulnerability-cve-2021-44228/
